Malaysia VIP Delegation Visit to Korea

Seoul Technical Inspection → IEG Headquarters Stop → Cheonan Visit & Dinner → Seoul Overnight → Next-Morning Departure to Gumi
Prepared by IEG · Version 1.0

Executive Summary

This program is designed to create a seamless and impactful flow from technology validation to practical implementation, culminating in relationship building. The delegation will begin the morning verifying technological capabilities through AI-based robotics in Suseo and autonomous mobility in Pangyo. Following a brief host transition stop at IEG Headquarters, the afternoon shifts to Cheonan for a direct review of hands-on engineering education and equipment adoption at KOREATECH. The day concludes with a refined official welcome dinner in Cheonan, followed by a comfort-oriented return to Seoul for a premium overnight stay. This sequence guarantees the delegation departs from Seoul Station to Gumi the next morning fully aligned and well-rested.

Key Management Message
The delegation builds technological trust in the morning, verifies implementation proof in the afternoon, and solidifies partnership commitment in the evening.

Premium Networking Lunch (Pangyo)

A dedicated networking lunch in Pangyo with stakeholders. Must feature a halal-friendly menu in a private room with no alcohol. Executive seating notes: the principal host should be seated to the right of the delegation lead, with the interpreter positioned adjacent.

Official Welcome Dinner (Cheonan — Option B)

Refined Korean dinner immediately following the KOREATECH visit. Reminder: Pre-confirm dietary requirements; strict no-pork/no-lard/no-alcohol management.

Examples — Not Confirmed Reservations:
  • Songyeon: Korean course meal / hanjeongsik in Yuryang-dong, Cheonan.
  • Surabistro: Modern Korean fine dining in Buldang-dong, Cheonan.
  • Mountain Range: Korean restaurant in Anseo-dong, Cheonan, with vegetable/tofu options.

KORAIL Mini Travel Note
Tickets can be purchased from one month before departure up to a short cutoff before departure on the official KORAIL foreigner site. It is highly recommended to pre-book all KTX seats and provide platform guidance for foreign delegates to ensure smooth station navigation.

Quick Morning Departure Sequence

  1. Wake-up & Luggage: Final room check and luggage consolidation.
  2. Checkout & Loading: Coordinated hotel checkout and luggage loading into vehicles.
  3. Station Transfer: Transfer to Seoul Station ensuring a comfortable traffic buffer.
  4. Platform Navigation: Escorted navigation directly to the KTX boarding platform.
  5. Boarding: KTX boarding, luggage stowage, and final seat confirmation.
Goal: calm, punctual, and low-friction departure to Gumi.
