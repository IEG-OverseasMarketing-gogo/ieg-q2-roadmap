OVERVIEW

Program Summary & Equipment Overview

This document presents the Competency Mapping Matrix and Detailed Module Contents for the IEG Smart Factory TVET Program — a 32-week, 960-hour training curriculum designed to prepare BOP youth for employment in the Philippine electronics and manufacturing sector.

The program is structured into two sequential phases: EIM NC II (Weeks 1–16, 480 hours) covering foundational electrical, pneumatic, and PLC competencies; and EIM NC III (Weeks 17–32, 480 hours) covering advanced PLC programming, hydraulics, industrial instrumentation, and smart factory systems. All modules are delivered through hands-on equipment-based training using IEG's six-product training lineup.

Training Equipment Used:
IEG 6030 — Electric Machine Trainer IEG 4000 — Pneumatics Trainer IEG 3430 — Mechatronics Trainer II IEG 3432 — Mechatronics Trainer I IEG 4100 — Hydraulics Trainer IEG 6050 — Smart Factory System
