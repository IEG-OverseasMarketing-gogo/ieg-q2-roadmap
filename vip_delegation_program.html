Malaysia VIP Delegation Visit to Korea
Seoul Technical Inspection · Official Welcome Dinner · Next-Day Departure from Seoul Station to Gumi
Executive Program Overview
This program is designed to create a seamless flow from technology validation to education-based implementation and finally to relationship building through premium Korean hospitality. The delegation will begin with technical inspections in Seoul, continue with a practical education and equipment adoption visit in Cheonan, and conclude the day with a premium welcome dinner and overnight stay in Seoul before departing from Seoul Station the next morning.
Key Management Message
The delegation builds technological trust in the morning through robotics in Suseo and autonomous mobility in Pangyo, verifies implementation potential in the afternoon through practical engineering education in Cheonan, and strengthens partnership commitment in the evening through premium Korean hospitality in Seoul.

Program Logic
The program is intentionally sequenced from technology to implementation to partnership. Morning visits establish trust, the Cheonan program proves applicability, and the Seoul evening schedule strengthens emotional connection.
